Our Lara

Vignan's Lara Institute of Technology & Science was established with the aim of providing quality technical education on par with international standards at Vadlamudi, Guntur District in the year 2007. It is approved by All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi, and affiliated to Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University Kakinada, Kakinada. The college is located in the green, transgenic, and rural sylvan environs of Vadlamudi, on the Guntur-Tenali highway, about 14 km from Guntur and 11 km from Tenali. The college is a virtual haven of rural quiet and idyllic beauty spread over a large area. The splendid avenue and sprawling playgrounds and the verdure in and around the campus make it one of the most preferred choices for the aspirants of Engineering studies. The college has well qualified young and experienced Ph.D. and M.Tech staff supported by Technical Staff.

Vision & Mission

To evolve into a Centre of Excellence in Science and Technology for producing globally acclaimed professionals who are psychologically strong, emotionally balanced, and guided by social consciousness and ethical values.

Mission:

  • To strive for excellence in teaching-learning and evaluation through innovative practices.
  • To equip students with problem-solving abilities through industrial interaction, novel, and high-end laboratory work.

Academic Council

Functions of the Academic Council

  • To scrutinize and approve the proposals with or without modification of the Board of Studies regarding courses of study, academic regulations, curricula, syllabi, and modifications thereof.
  • To make regulations regarding the admission of students to different programmes of study in the Autonomous College, keeping in view the policy of the Government.
  • To make regulations for sports, extra-curricular activities, and proper maintenance and functioning of the playgrounds and hostels.
  • To recommend to the Governing Body proposals for the institution of new programmes of study.
  • To recommend to the Governing Body the institution of scholarships, studentships, fellowships, prizes, and medals, and to frame regulations for the award of the same.
  • To advise the Governing Body on suggestions(s) pertaining to academic affairs.
  • To perform such other functions as may be assigned by the Governing Body.

Term and Meetings

The term of the nominated members shall be three years. Meetings of the Academic Council shall be held at least once every six months.

Board of Studies

The Board of Studies is responsible for designing the curriculum and ensuring its relevance to industry needs. It reviews and recommends courses, syllabi, and academic regulations to the Academic Council for approval. The board comprises experienced faculty members, industry experts, and alumni who contribute to shaping the academic framework of the institute.

The board meets regularly to assess the effectiveness of the curriculum and make necessary adjustments based on feedback from students and industry trends. It also encourages faculty development programs to keep the teaching staff updated with the latest developments in technology and education.

Campus Placements

Vignan's Lara Institute of Technology and Science boasts a dedicated placement cell that works tirelessly to ensure that students secure meaningful employment before they graduate. The college has established strong ties with various companies and organizations across different sectors, facilitating a wide array of job opportunities for its students.

The placement cell organizes pre-placement training sessions that include resume writing workshops, mock interviews, and soft skills development, all aimed at enhancing the employability of students. Companies that have previously visited the campus include major tech giants like TCS, Wipro, Infosys, and more, providing students with ample opportunities to kickstart their careers.

The average placement rate at V-LITS is impressive, with many students receiving multiple job offers. The college also emphasizes internships and industrial training, allowing students to gain practical experience and exposure to real-world challenges. This comprehensive approach ensures that graduates are well-prepared to meet the demands of the job market.

The college's commitment to maintaining industry relationships and continuously improving its curriculum ensures that students are equipped with the skills and knowledge necessary to thrive in their chosen fields.
